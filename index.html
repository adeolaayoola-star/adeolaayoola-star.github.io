About

About me

Seven-plus years building genomic pipelines that turn raw sequencing into research- and clinical-grade data. I'm currently a Project Scientist in the Department of Human Genetics at UCLA, running variant-calling, QC, and population-structure pipelines on cohorts of 1,200+ samples. My path runs through a PhD in Genetics at the Chinese Academy of Sciences and a postdoctoral fellowship at Duke University. In production environments, I've cut analytical error rates by 25% and lifted pipeline throughput by 40%.

My toolkit includes GATK, ANGSD, PLINK, ADMIXTURE, and Snakemake on SLURM, AWS, and Google Cloud, paired with SOP design and regulatory-aligned documentation. I've published in Molecular Biology and Evolution, Scientific Reports, and Frontiers in Genetics, with grant support from the National Geographic Society, the Fonseca Species Conservation Fund, and the Rufford Foundation. I work fluently across academic research programs and industry teams in biotech and clinical genomics.

Research

Research areas

I work across three areas: population genomics, eDNA-based biodiversity and disease surveillance, and ecological modeling. The methods carry across human cohorts, primate populations, and field environments.

01

Population genomics and adaptation

Whole-genome sequencing, demographic modeling, selection scans, and admixture inference applied to human and non-human populations across African landscapes, including signatures of malaria resistance and pigmentation variation along environmental gradients.

WGS / WESGATKADMIXTUREPCAngsdBEAST
02

eDNA, iDNA and biomolecular surveillance

Non-invasive environmental and invertebrate DNA methods for biodiversity inference and disease surveillance, including Oxford Nanopore field sequencing deployed in UNESCO forests and zoonotic-risk landscapes.

eDNA / iDNANanoporeMetabarcodingOne Health
03

Human and environmental modeling

Species distribution and ecological niche modeling to reconstruct how climate change, habitat fragmentation, and land-use transformation shape biological diversity and public-health outcomes at the landscape scale.

MaxEntArcGISQGISNiche modeling
Skills

Skills and expertise

Seven-plus years of training across wet-lab, computational, and field-based research, in laboratories in China, Germany, Côte d'Ivoire, and the United States.

Genomics and NGS

Sequencing · Variant calling · QC
  • Whole-genome and exome sequencing (WGS / WES)
  • Variant calling: GATK, ANGSD, BCFtools, Samtools
  • QC pipelines: FastQC, MultiQC, Trimmomatic
  • Wet-lab: DNA/RNA extraction, primer design, qPCR
  • Oxford Nanopore sequencing and real-time field ID

Population and statistical genetics

Structure · Diversity · Selection
  • PLINK, ADMIXTURE, PCAngsd, STRUCTURE, BEAST
  • FST, PCA, LD analysis, demographic inference
  • Selection scans on low-depth sequencing data
  • Genotype-likelihood frameworks (ANGSD)
  • GWAS and genotype modeling on clinical cohorts

Computing and workflow

HPC · Cloud · Automation
